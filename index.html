Wells, NY

I lived in a small town, near Canada in the Adirondack Mountains. Wells is a very small town, one road makes up the main street that brings you in and out of this small town.

Amsterdam, NY

In the summer of 2003, I lived in this small town about halfway between Grand Rapids and Lake Michigan. Summers in Michagans are a dream, with their long days, relatively mild temps, and plenty of outdoor recreation at close access.

Anguilla, BVI

Back in 2016, I moved to the Caribbean, I was working at the 4 Seasons hotel, as a building engineer. The hotel had acquired a new property and needed people to help train the staff and open the hotel. I was lucky enough to live there for over a month!
